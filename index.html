About G.S Mwulire II

G.S Mwulire II is a comprehensive educational institution located in Rwamagana District, Eastern Province of Rwanda. Since our establishment, we have been committed to providing quality education to students from nursery to ordinary level.

Our mission is to nurture well-rounded individuals with strong academic foundations, ethical values, and the skills needed to succeed in a rapidly changing world. We maintain small class sizes to ensure personalized attention for each student.
